> PRIVACY PROTOCOLS

Status: PUBLIC LEDGER ACTIVE

1. DATA COLLECTION

BitZero prioritizes decentralization and anonymity. We do not collect personal identifying information (Name, Email, Phone) unless you voluntarily provide it.

Data we DO process:

  • Public Wallet Addresses: Visible when you connect via Web3.
  • Transaction Data: All interactions with our Smart Contracts are publicly and permanently recorded on the Base Blockchain.
  • Technical Logs: Standard server logs (IP, Browser type) may be temporarily processed for website security (e.g., DDOS protection).

2. BLOCKCHAIN TRANSPARENCY

Please be aware that the Base Blockchain is a public ledger. Any transaction you execute involving $BIT, staking, or nodes is permanent and viewable by anyone. We cannot "delete" data from the blockchain.

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Our interface may utilize and link to third-party services:

  • RPC Nodes: Used to read and transmit blockchain data.
  • Wallet Providers: (e.g., MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet). Their respective privacy policies apply when you use their software.
  • Basescan: For public block and transaction viewing.

4. COOKIES AND LOCAL STORAGE

We use minimal local storage to save front-end preferences (e.g., UI settings). We do not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels.

5. SECURITY

While we secure our front-end interface, you are solely responsible for safeguarding your private keys and seed phrases. BitZero developers can never recover your wallet if you lose access.
